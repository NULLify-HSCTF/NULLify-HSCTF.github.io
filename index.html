What is NULLify?

NULLify is the University of Nebraska Omaha’s Cybersecurity student club and organization. We meet twice a week and provide both introductory and advanced cybersecurity talks and topics. Hands-on workshops help students gain practical experience and discover what they love most!

NULLify actively participates in Capture The Flag competitions and has received awards in the past.

What is HSCTF?

High School Capture The Flag (HSCTF) is a beginner-friendly CTF competition hosted annually by NULLify. Students compete in teams to solve jeopardy-style cybersecurity challenges.

Students gain hands-on experience with tools and labs at the Peter Kiewit Institute on Scott Campus. Great for anyone interested in cybersecurity or IT!
